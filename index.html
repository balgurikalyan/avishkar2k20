ABOUT US



INTRODUCTION:

Avishkar, the flagship event of IEEE - VBIT SB, is a technical Paper Presentation competition held exclusively for the Freshmen of VBIT. It assists in envisaging the innovative insight of the first-year students and ameliorating their technical dexterity. IEEE - VBIT SB, was awarded the coveted ‘Darrel Chong Gold Student Activity Award’ in the year 2011 for conducting Avishkar consecutively for five years and it has been one of the most prominent events conducted since then. Avishkar bolsters to imbibe a competitive spirit and aids students to become better and come out as confident individuals.



What is Our Goal?

Avishkar is a technical Paper Presentation held exclusively for first-year students of VBIT. It is an ideal platform for the young minds to get exposure to various technical facets. Students here exhibit their innovative ideas through their PowerPoint Presentation. It consists of two rounds:

Round 1: Preliminary round

Round 2: Final round

In the preliminary round, the participants are segregated based upon their domains into the respective halls for their presentations. The duration of each presentation is 10 minutes, which includes 8 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes allotted for the queries session. According to their performance and other criteria, the top 10 teams are selected and sent to the final round. The top ten teams compete in the final round. The best teams are then judged accordingly and felicitated.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

Avishkar being a technical event conducted by IEEE - VBIT SB, must pursue a set of paradigms which are as follows:

Abstract:
1) The font should be Times New Roman.
2) The font size of the content must be 10.
3) Content should be justified.
4) Mention your Email ID in Italics.
5) Divide the content into two columns.
6) Word limit must not exceed 200-250 words.
...
Important note:
• Only if the abstract is submitted, you will be provided with the sample PPT in IEEE format.
• Submit your abstract only as a word document i.e. in the format of .docx.
• Name your abstract as follows: ‘name_last3digitsofyourrollnumber’ Example: Srinivas_123_Samhith_045.docx PowerPoint Presentation [PPT]:
• The minimum number of slides in the PPT is 10.
• The maximum number of slides in the PPT is 15.
• No animations or special effects are allowed in the PPT.
• The font in the PPT should be Times New Roman.
• The first slide of the PPT must contain your Name and Roll Number.
• The PPT should be professional.
How can we be a part of Avishkar?
Follow these steps to be part of Avishkar 2K20.
1) Register yourself online through the registration link or by scanning the QR code provide on the poster and website.
NOTE: There is no registration fee.
2) Choose the category mentioned below:
• PPT: You can participate individually or in a team of two.
NOTE: Avoid generic topics.
3) After completing the registration process, select any technical topic of your choice.
4) Prepare the abstract for the technical topic selected.
5) After completion and submission of the abstract, prepare and submit the PPT.

EVENT FAQs

I am interested in participating, but don't know what to do ?

The Organizing Committee will help you. You can contact them from the contacts section mentioned on the web

What kind of topics should we choose?

The topic that you select should be a technical topic.

What is the participation fee?

There is no registration fee, it is free for everyone.

What if I do not have a facility to work?

In that case we will provide you with the required facility in the lab.

Can I select a team member from another branch?

Yes, you are not restricted to any branches. You can team up with any first-year student irrespective of their branch.
